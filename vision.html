Vision & Mission

Our Goals That Move Us Forward

VISION

OSCARS Studies Technical & Skill Education Center aims to be an outstanding institution for talent development and knowledge creation for a vibrant and inclusive society. Everybody comes to this educational institute for computer education.

MISSION

The primary purpose of OSCARS Studies Technical & Skill Education Computer Center is to provide a learning environment in which faculty, staff, and students work together for quality computer education.

Oscars Studies Technical & Skill Education Pvt. Ltd. provides Distance Education, HP SOS Examination Support and University Courses along with top-quality skill-based vocational training across Himachal Pradesh.
